Entry 10 — Understanding

Conclusion

"Culture is that complex whole which includes knowledge, belief, art, morals, law, customs and any other capabilities and habits acquired by man as a member of society." Edward Burnett Tylor, Globalization and Culture: Global MélangeWhat is culuture?

To finish off this blog post series, I want to share my personal thoughts on globalisation. I think that globalisation is ingrained in our human DNA. We are all migrants and we all have been doing an exchange of everything we are for centuries. Although war, division and hate may spread accross the world, I think that love, support and the ability to share cultures will prevail.

As the quote describes, everything from knowledge all the way to moral capabilities of man is proof that we are human.
